OFFLINE EXPLORES HOW MODERN CONNECTION IS INCREASINGLY MEDIATED THROUGH SCREENS. THROUGH STAGED PHOTOGRAPHIC SCENES, THE PROJECT REFLECTS ON MOMENTS WHERE PEOPLE ARE PHYSICALLY PRESENT YET DIGITALLY DISTRACTED.

BY IMAGINING A TEMPORARY ABSENCE OF THE INTERNET, THE WORK REVEALS HOW INDIVIDUALS MIGHT RECONNECT WITH THEIR SURROUNDINGS AND WITH EACH OTHER, ULTIMATELY QUESTIONING HOW TECHNOLOGY SHAPES THE WAY WE EXPERIENCE HUMAN INTERACTION.
